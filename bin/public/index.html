Finnish Pancake (Pannukakku)

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F (220Â°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Gradually add flour and salt, mixing until smooth.
  4. Pour melted butter into a baking dish.
  5. Pour the batter over the butter and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven when golden brown and puffed.
  7. Let cool slightly, then serve with jam or fresh berries.
